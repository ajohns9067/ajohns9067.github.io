Calculation Outline

Traits

The effect of traits on skill gain is very subjective. Emotional traits have measurable effects on skill gain, like Creative inducing an inspired mood, which boosts creative skills(1), but other traits like Art Lover obviously affect which career a Sim might or should go into, but how do you quantify the effect of this trait? Some traits boost the coefficient for certain skills, and some provide a score increase to certain careers' final score. Many traits do not affect any careers or skills needed for careers.

Skills

A Sim's current skills were used for the base of the skill intercept.

Likes/Dislikes (Preferences)

A Sim's preferences were used to augment the coefficient of a skill (either positively or negatively).

Aspirations

Aspirations include a variety of requirements, including attaining specific skill or career levels. The progression of these requirements over the course of the aspiration is used to generate a linear regression, which is added to the linear regression for associated skills.
